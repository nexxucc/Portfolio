Probabilistic Market Regime Detection using MACD and Astronomical Phase Modeling

Developed a machine-learning framework to probabilistically detect bullish momentum regime transitions in major equity indices by combining MACD-based technical indicators with deterministic astronomical phase features derived from planetary ephemeris data. The model was rigorously validated using walk-forward backtesting, statistical benchmarks, and event-based economic analysis to demonstrate improved regime-timing accuracy over traditional technical-indicator-only approaches.
